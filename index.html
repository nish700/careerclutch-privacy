1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. This is used for authentication and to personalize your experience.

Resume Data

When you upload or paste a resume for optimization, the text is processed on your device. Before any AI analysis, our on-device PII scrubber removes personal identifiers including:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Home address, Aadhaar number, PAN number
  • LinkedIn/GitHub URLs, date of birth
  • Family details (S/o, D/o, Father's/Mother's name)

Only the anonymized professional content (skills, experience, education) is sent to our AI provider for analysis. Your original unredacted resume is stored only on your device in local storage.

Interview Data

Your interview answers, scores, and performance metrics are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our cloud database (Supabase) for cross-device access. This data is associated with your account ID, not your personal identity.

Camera & Microphone

CareerClutch requests camera and microphone permissions for the following features:

Permission Feature How It's Used
CAMERA Video Interview Mode Records video during practice to analyze body language (eye contact, posture, expressions). Video frames are processed by AI and immediately deleted. No video is stored or transmitted.
MICROPHONE Voice Interview Mode Converts your spoken answers to text using on-device speech recognition. Audio is not recorded, stored, or sent to any server.

Usage & Analytics

We collect anonymized usage data through Firebase Analytics to improve the app. This includes: features used, session duration, crash reports, and device type. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
